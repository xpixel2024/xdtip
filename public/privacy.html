Privacy Policy

> LAST_MODIFIED: JAN 18, 2026

At XDTIP (accessible via https://xdtip.in), we recognize that trust is the currency of the creator economy. We are steadfastly committed to safeguarding the digital sovereignty and personal information of our creators and their community of supporters.

This Privacy Policy outlines the technical and procedural protocols we employ to collect, utilize, and protect your data.

1. Information Architecture & Data Collection

To provide our monetization infrastructure, we collect specific data points categorized as follows:

  • Identity Verification Data: Full Name, Email Address, and Channel Identity (required for account authentication and security).
  • Financial Routing Data: Unified Payments Interface (UPI) IDs. Note: This data is strictly utilized for the processing of payouts.
  • Transactional Metadata: Payment timestamps, gross amounts, and supporter messages required for the generation of "Tip Alerts."
Non-Storage Policy: XDTIP does not store sensitive payment instrument details (Credit Card/Debit Card numbers) on our servers. All processing is offloaded to PCI-DSS compliant gateways (e.g., Razorpay).

2. Operational Use of Data

We leverage your data exclusively for the following operational objectives:

  • Service Delivery: To facilitate the instant settlement of funds from supporter to creator via UPI rails.
  • Real-Time Overlay Generation: To trigger visual and auditory alerts (TTS) on your livestream via our WebSocket architecture.
  • Fraud Detection: To monitor transactional velocity and prevent chargeback fraud or money laundering attempts.
  • Compliance: To adhere to Indian financial regulations and digital tax reporting requirements.

3. Data Sovereignty & Sharing Protocol

We adhere to a strict "Need-to-Know" sharing policy. XDTIP does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to advertisers or data brokers.

Data is shared strictly with:

  • Payment Processors: (e.g., Razorpay) solely to execute financial transactions.
  • Legal Authorities: Only when compelled by a valid court order or to investigate illegal activity within the platform.

4. Security Infrastructure

We employ enterprise-grade security measures to protect your data in transit and at rest:

  • SSL Encryption: All data transmission between your browser and our servers is encrypted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology.
  • Database Isolation: User financial data is isolated from public-facing application logic.
  • Access Control: Internal access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel with multi-factor authentication.

5. Your Rights

As a user of the XDTIP platform, you retain the right to request the deletion of your account and associated data from our servers. To initiate a data erasure request, please contact our Data Protection Officer at connect@xdfun.in.
