Privacy Policy

> LAST_MODIFIED: APRIL 09, 2026

At XDTIP (accessible via https://xdtip.in), we provide a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform designed to enhance creator engagement through real-time broadcast tools. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our software users and their communities.

This Privacy Policy outlines how our software handles data to provide interactive overlays and broadcast telemetry.

1. Information Architecture & Software Data

To provide our engagement suite and manage user accounts, we collect data categorized as follows:

  • Account Credentials: Full Name and Email Address required for account authentication and software access.
  • Creator Identity: Stream channel links or usernames to sync broadcast overlays with the correct live feed.
  • Service Metadata: Information required to process platform service fees and maintain active software subscriptions.
  • Interaction Data: Timestamps and viewer-generated messages used solely to trigger real-time visual alerts and telemetry on the creator's overlay.
Data Security Notice: XDTIP is a software provider. We do not act as a payment processor. All financial transactions initiated via third-party integrations are handled by PCI-DSS compliant gateways (e.g., Razorpay/Cashfree). We do not store credit card or bank credentials.

2. Operational Use of Software Data

Data is utilized exclusively to maintain the performance and reliability of the engagement suite:

  • Software Delivery: To provision personalized broadcast dashboards and unique overlay URLs.
  • Real-Time Alert Triggering: To execute visual and auditory alerts (TTS) on livestreams via our WebSocket architecture.
  • Platform Analytics: To provide creators with engagement metrics and software usage history.
  • System Integrity: To monitor for bot activity or malicious attempts to disrupt software services.

3. Data Sharing & Third-Party Integrations

XDTIP does not sell or trade user data. Data is only shared when necessary to provide the core software experience:

  • Integration Partners: Data required by broadcast platforms or payment gateways to sync alerts and verify platform service fees.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by Indian law to prevent unauthorized use of digital services.

4. Technical Safeguards

We employ modern encryption protocols to protect software data:

  • SSL Encryption: All traffic between the user and the dashboard is secured via industry-standard SSL.
  • Overlay Security: Unique, obfuscated URLs for broadcast overlays to prevent unauthorized access to creator widgets.
  • Internal Access: Restricted internal access to user accounts, limited to authorized technical support personnel.

5. User Rights & Data Portability

Users maintain full control over their account data. You may request account closure or data erasure at any time by contacting our support uplink at connect@xdfun.in.
